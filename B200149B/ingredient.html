Aarón Sánchez Owner of Johnny Sánchez

Good health is inherent to the cuisine. It is a true love of food, wary of the trappings of gluttony or breathless excess. If you were to design a perfect diet based on what modern science tells us today, it might look a lot like the way Japan has been eating for centuries.

Scott Conant Owner of 5 Scarpetta restaurants
